Incremental RPG. Level your heroes to progress through the zones. No offline progress, automatic saves every 30 seconds.
Each hero level gives +3 base health, and extra damage to attacks (healing for Shaman). If a hero dies, it cannot do damage or heal and must be revived. Passive health/damage/gold bonuses are still active upon death.
If every hero dies, you will be set back 5 zones.
After zone 100 you can Rebirth, starting at zone 1 keeping your Relics. You can progress past zone 100 to potentially find better Relics. Not added yet

Shade:
A melee damage hero with two attacks: Stab and Slit.
Shade's active skill is Barricade which blocks all incoming damage for 30 seconds, 120 second cooldown.

Shaman:
A healing support hero with two skills: Heal and Vigor. Heal adds a healing buff to all heroes. Vigor buffs the % health of all heroes.
Shaman's active skill is Resurrect which revives all dead heroes, 120 second cooldown.

Feu:
A fire spell damage hero with two attacks: Flare and Meteor.
Shade's active skill is Enrage triples all hero damage for 30 seconds, 180 second cooldown.

Clout:
A general support hero with two skills: Potential and Affluence. Potential adds a % damage buff to all heroes. Affluence buffs the % gold of all enemy kills.
Shaman's active skill is Soothe triples all healing effects for 30 seconds, 180 second cooldown.

Flux:
A projectile damage hero with two attacks: Bombard and Pierce.
Shade's active skill is Converge combines all hero attacks and unleashes them at once, 60 second cooldown.
Tip: use Enrage before triggering Converge.


A Relic drops each time you reach a new highest zone. Each Relic provides a different bonus to the whole team.
Relics can roll with better stats the higher level you reach, but the rolls are random. You may not always find an upgrade.

Relics provide:
% Damage
% Health
Base Damage
% Gold
% Life Steal (to Shade/Feu/Flux attacks).

You can use Old Relics to purchase upgrades; Cooldown Reduction, and Cost Reduction. Not added yet
